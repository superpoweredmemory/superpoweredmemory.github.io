Superpowered Memory

A Course on How To Master Your Memory And Become an Effective Learner

Imagine how much more you could accomplish if you could remember everything you learn.

What impact would that have in your job, in your studies, throughout your life?

Knowing how to learn is one of the foundational skills to get ahead in this fast changing age - it is the pillar that allows you to excel at your goals and to keep you one step ahead of the competition, robotic or otherwise.

In Superpowered Memory you will learn how to leverage the research findings of how memory works into the techniques and practices that will develop you into a much more effective learner. You will put away with the inefective techniques everyone uses and learn what research has found to actually work.

My goal is to teach you not only how to learn better, but smarter.

You will learn:

How your our memory works and what this implies about how we should think about how to structure our learning

The science of how we forget and the techniques that will allow you to create memories that last forever

How to use Spaced Repetition Systems to make use of the benefit of spacing and testing

Techniques to make sure you aren't fooled by yourself into thinking you've learned something when you haven't

Tips on how to develop what you're learning into habits you use throughout your life

With Superpowered Memory you will learn the framework behind effective learning that will allow you to plan your learning in the smartest way possible. You will learn at a much faster rate while at the same time that you retain much more information than before, enhancing your performance in any area of your life that depends on the knowledge you're able to bring to the table.
